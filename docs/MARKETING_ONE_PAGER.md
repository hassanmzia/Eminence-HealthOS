## The AI Operating System for Digital Healthcare Platforms

---

### THE PROBLEM

Healthcare organizations run **5+ disconnected systems** for RPM, telehealth, scheduling, analytics, and billing. This fragmentation costs the US healthcare system **$1.35 trillion annually** in administrative waste, causes clinician burnout (86% report symptoms), and prevents holistic patient care.

---

### THE SOLUTION

**Eminence HealthOS** is a unified AI-powered healthcare platform where **30 specialized AI agents** coordinate to deliver intelligent, automated clinical workflows — replacing disconnected point solutions with one intelligent operating system.
